Mapping Technology in Wilderness Search and Rescue

a master's thesis by Caroline Rose

I conducted an interview study with wilderness search and rescue specialists to find out how they use mapping technology, what challenges they encounter with mapping technology, and how mapping technology might be improved to better support searches.

This website is designed to host my thesis manuscript and related materials.
