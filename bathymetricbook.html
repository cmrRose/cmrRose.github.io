Bathymetric Book

next

A relief map in book form, showing the underwater contours of Crater Lake in Oregon. The vertical and horizontal scales are identical, making it a scale model of the lake floor. This book earned three awards at the Wisconsin Land Information Association Conference in 2013: Best in Category, Best Student Map, and People's Choice. It then received the Barbara Bartz Petchenik Undergraduate Award in Cartography, 1st Place for 2012-2013 from the UW-Madison Geography Department.
