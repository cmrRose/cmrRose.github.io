Magnetic Mendota

next

This project was a unique and challenging map design for the Wisconsin Hoofers, the outdoor clubs at the University of Wisconsin-Madison. The result is a three-dimensional map of Lake Mendota with a magnetic surface which functions as a teaching tool and enhances the aesthetic of a newly renovated space in the University of Wisconsin’s student union building. It is designed to accommodate a wide variety of changing needs for map labels.
